Biography

Inskip Harrison was born on 16 September 1812, most likely in England, and came to America as part of the early wave of English settlers who would establish what became known as the English Settlement in Rome Township, Crawford County, Pennsylvania.

His name — Inskip — became one of the most enduring in the Harrison family line. It was carried forward by his son Edward Inskip Harrison, appeared again in Jane Inskip Harrison a generation before him, and crossed into the Morris family through marriage, appearing still in Harold Inskip Morris decades after Inskip's death. Few names in this cemetery tell a family story so clearly.

Inskip Harrison died on 25 November 1888, aged 76, and was among the early burials in what would become Harrison Cemetery — a cemetery that would eventually bear his family's name.

Additional biography will be added as monument examination and archival research deepens. Immigration date, occupation, and community role are under active research. If you have information about Inskip Harrison, please use the contribution form below.
